Rabbits

Rabbits are tiny and furry animals popularly known as bunnies. Rabbits have sharp front teeth to cut thick vegetables and leaves. Rabbits are adorable little animals. Everything from their soft fur, cute appearance and funny mannerisms make them lovable pet animals. Rabbits or bunnies are popular pet animals.


Rabbits in the wild live in colonies called warrens. They are frequently preyed upon by eagles, big cats, foxes and snakes. They live both, in the wild and as pets. Rabbits are popular as pets among animal lovers.
